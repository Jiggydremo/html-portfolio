The Best Movies According to Ebuka

My Top Three Movies of All time.

Interstella

It was a very emotional film for me as the main lead wasnt there to watch his daughter grow up rather he sacrificed those moments for survival of humanity. Overall ill say its a 10/10 movie.

Inception

Another great work by christopher nolan, had me on the edge of my seat anytime they entered another person's dream and the subconscious started fighting back. Overall a wonderful experience and a 9/10 movie.

Whiplash

Oh boy when i tell you that this movie was a masterpiece i mean it. The zeal Andrew Neiman had to become the best amd the determination to be at the top , with his instructor Terence Fletcher equally known for his teaching talents as for his terrifying methods. Overall a 10/10 movie.
